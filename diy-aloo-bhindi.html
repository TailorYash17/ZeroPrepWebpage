🔥 Heat the Oil ⬇️ Prep

Take 3/4th amount of oil from pouch 1 (Butter/Oil) in a pan. Heat on medium flame until the oil shimmers (about 1 minute). This indicates the oil is ready for sautéing.

🔥 Heat until oil shimmers
2️⃣

🥔 Fry the Potatoes ⬇️ Cook

Take bite-sized pieces from pouch 2 (Cut Potato) and fry in oil until golden. Drain and set aside.

🟠 Fry until golden
3️⃣

🥬 Fry the Okra ⬇️ Cook

In same pan fry okra from pouch 3 till fully cooked and crisp. Set aside for later addition.

💧 Keep okra dry
4️⃣

🧅 Sauté Onions ⬇️ Sauté

Heat rest of the oil in the pan. Add sliced onions and sauté, stirring occasionally, until translucent or light brown.

↻ Stir until translucent
5️⃣

🧄 Add Ginger-Garlic ⬇️ Sauté

Add 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste; sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.

👃 Cook until fragrant
6️⃣

🍅 Add Tomatoes ⬇️ Cook

Add 1 cup finely chopped tomatoes; cook, stirring occasionally, until soft and pulpy.

🍅 Cook until soft
7️⃣

🧴 Add Spice Box 1 ⬇️ Spice

Add Spice Box 1. Stir well and cook 1–2 minutes until the oil begins to separate from the masala.

🍽️ Cook until oil separates
8️⃣

🥗 Add Vegetables & Finish ⬇️ Finish

Add the fried potatoes and sautéed okra to the masala. Gently fold so the masala coats both vegetables evenly. Cook for 1 minute. Add Spice Box 2; mix thoroughly and sauté for another minute to finish.

↻ Fold gently
🍽️

Finish & Serve

Taste and adjust salt/spices if needed. Garnish with chopped coriander or a squeeze of lemon (optional). Serve hot with rotis, paratha, rice or as a stuffing for wraps.

Quick Tips

💧 Keep Okra Dry

Pat okra completely dry before frying to avoid sliminess.

🌶️ Tangy Twist

Add a pinch of amchur (dry mango powder) with Spice Box 2.

👨‍🍳 Separate Frying

Fry potatoes and okra separately to keep textures crisp.
